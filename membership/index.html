Access & Discounts

Swimming at Confederation Leisure Centre

Our members can swim free of charge during public swim times or lane swim times at Confederation Leisure Centre, 11204-43 Ave. Just present your valid 2016/17 EPCL membership at the pool to get a City Recreation Facilities card charged with 12 free swims. Valid only for Confederation Pool and limited to family members listed on your membership card. Call 311 or go to www.edmonton.ca/activities_parks_ recreation/ confederation-drop- in-pool-schedule.aspx for pool hours.
